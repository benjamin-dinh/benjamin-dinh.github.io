About Me

Nice to meet you, I'm Benjamin! I'm a third year at University of California, Davis studying Computer Science and minoring in Communication. My interests lie in making technology widely accessible and delivering unique product experiences.

It has been a long-standing goal of mine to use technology for the greater good of society. In an age of social media, it can be overwhelming to see software exploit human tendencies. I want to turn this around and build products that will enhance user experiences and truly make our lives better.

Say hi over on LinkedIn or email, I would love to chat!
